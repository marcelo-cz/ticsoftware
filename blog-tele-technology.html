Messaging Product Suite – 'Tele' Technology with TIC Software

📅 December 29, 2022
✍️ Phil Ly
Messaging Modernization

Automating & Report Delivery via Email, Web, Fax and File Transfer

Tele is a prefix that combines the meaning of the phases "reaching over a distance," "carried out between two remote points," and "performed or operated through electronic transmissions." The 'Tele' family group technologies of TIC Software – has been designed for application modernization.

These Messaging products are designed to seamlessly integrate NonStop systems with other platforms via format conversion and data delivery – enabling automated processing and delivery of reports and information and keeping the applications organizations running smoothly.

TeleMail

TeleMail gives NonStop users to deliver information via the Internet to virtually anywhere. Reports and documents archived on the server may be delivered as either an email attachment or in the message itself. Detailed log files and distribution lists may also be distributed for status and statistical reporting.

TeleMail can automatically translate information into a variety of formats including PDF, HTML and ZIP files; and requires no external hardware or software as it runs on the server. A Secure option is also available to support SSL and TLS SMTP Protocol.

TeleWeb

TeleWeb enable spooler reports to be viewed via a Web browser. Secure data access allows online viewing of server information as soon as data is available. Data may be converted into a number of formats such as PDF, HTML and TXT; and distributed to individuals or groups – securely and automatically. TeleWeb tracks distribution activity for accountability, and saves a secure copy of reports generated as a failsafe.

TeleFax

Even though fax is an old technology, it is far from being obsolete. Fax is still very important in the business world, because it is simple, legally binding, and secure. TIC's TeleFax application works with legacy systems to provide for automated server information delivery via fax server technology. This highly configurable system supports user-defined options for automated fax delivery timing, cover pages and priority levels – and tracks system status and fax transmission activity. System scalability and flexibility ensure that TeleFax can handle growth without any change to the application.

TeleFTP

NonStop spooler reports and files can be automatically transmitted via FTP protocol using TeleFTP freeing up IT time from having to manually configure and accomplish this task. TeleFTP provides for data to be converted into a variety of formats (TXT, PDF, ZIP) for delivery and flexible API is available for customizing for any environment.

Rock-solid legacy systems require application modernization to integrate and leverage the latest technology. TIC's 'Tele' line of software applications helps ensure that organization's using these servers for their 'mission-critical' day-to-day business operations can rest easy. Because TIC doesn't just offer software products and consulting services – we also offer solutions for your peace of mind.

Key Technologies

The Tele family encompasses several interconnected products:

  • TeleMail: Email integration and report delivery via SMTP
  • TeleWeb: Web browser access to spooler reports with secure data viewing
  • TeleFax: Automated fax delivery with configurable options
  • TeleFTP: FTP-based file transmission with format conversion

All of these products share the common goal of modernizing NonStop systems by enabling seamless integration with contemporary platforms and technologies, eliminating manual processes, and ensuring reliable, secure delivery of critical business information.

Conclusion

The 'Tele' family of products from TIC Software represents a comprehensive approach to NonStop modernization. By automating report and data delivery across multiple channels – email, web, fax, and FTP – organizations can reduce operational overhead, minimize human error, and ensure that critical information reaches the right people at the right time.

Whether you're looking to modernize your messaging infrastructure, automate report distribution, or integrate your NonStop systems with cloud-based solutions, TIC's Tele technologies provide proven, reliable solutions.
